THE FOUNDATION

Pramod aggarwal, the visionary behind THE EKUM, has crafted a legacy of luxury and architectural brilliance. Each design element at THE EKUM reflects his keen attention to detail. As a luminary in luxury real estate, he's not only raised iconic structures but also fostered profound trust and goodwill in South Delhi.

Shashank aggarwal, an alumnus of Shaheed Bhagat Singh College and the Indian School of Business, Hyderabad, stands as a light of innovative thinking at THE EKUM. Bringing fresh perspectives and modern strategies, he fortifies the journey towards architectural ingenuity and luxurious living. Under his visionary leadership, residences crafted by THE EKUM transcend convention, evolving into contemporary masterpieces that harmoniously merge tradition with the future's aspirations.

Emerging from South Delhi's narrative lanes, The Ekum is more than a name. It symbolizes unity, exceptional craftsmanship, and a vision consistently striving for excellence. Through dedication to customer satisfaction, adherence to regulations, and our rich heritage in real estate, we've solidified our position as market leaders. At The Ekum, we craft spaces where dreams thrive, always remembering: “WE ARE THE ONE.”

LEGACY

For over three and a half decades, THE EKUM has stood as a paragon of authenticity in the realm of luxury living. Beyond a brand, THE EKUM signifies unmatched craftsmanship and embodies unwavering trust. In an era where transient trends often dominated, THE EKUM remained resolute, championing unparalleled quality and quintessential luxury. Its 35-year legacy is not just a measure of time; it narrates a tale of steadfast consistency, an unyielding commitment, and the profound bond the brand shares with its esteemed clientele.

The visionary behind it all, Pramod Aggarwal, aspired to sculpt lasting legacies and nurture enduring relationships. Every design brought to life and home realized carries the very essence of THE EKUM. As it stands today, reflecting on its distinguished journey, THE EKUM assures a future where authenticity, trust, and legacy will remain its guiding principles.

WHY TRUST US ?

The future is EKUM, a philosophy deeply rooted in trust, craftsmanship, and innovation. Founded by Pramod Aggarwal, we craft sanctuaries where dreams blossom and memories are intricately woven. Our commitment to detail resonates perfection, with meticulous attention in every corner, believing that God is in every detail. We prioritize safety, comfort, and sustainability, ensuring each design embodies these ideals and nurtures the well-being of its residents.

Choosing THE EKUM is aligning with a legacy where every stone speaks of trust and dedication. Our properties are more than structures; they're reflections of unparalleled commitment, resonating with the aspirations of our clients. Join us, be part of Something Greater, Something One.
